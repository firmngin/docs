**firmngin.dev** is an **AIoT** (Artificial Intelligence \+ Internet of Things) platform for hardware businesses. One place to connect firmware to the cloud, monitor devices in real time, run automations, and manage paid services—from prototype to production.

## What is firmngin.dev?

Firmngin brings **device management**, **firmware & OTA**, **rule-based automation**, and **monetization** into a single workflow. You create a workspace, register boards (e.g. ESP32/ESP8266 or Raspberry Pi), integrate the official **Arduino library** or **Python SDK**, and operate devices from the dashboard—without stitching together multiple services.

The platform fits IoT product builders, hardware teams, and businesses that want to sell device access or metered services directly from firmware.

## What you can do

- **Connect devices** — generate MQTT credentials, publish telemetry and state, and see changes live in the dashboard or over WebSocket.
- **Control & automate** — configure entities, run rules/automations, and link device events to cloud or cross-device actions.
- **Deploy firmware** — prepare OTA artifacts and monitor rollouts across your device fleet.
- **Sell device services** — create merchants (sandbox and live), catalog items, link devices to merchants, and expose a public menu for end users.
- **Build integrations** — use the REST API, webhooks, MQTT topics, and API reference docs for your own systems.
