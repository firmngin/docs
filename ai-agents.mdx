Workspace AI Agents help you inspect devices, review logs, operate permitted controls, and manage automations with natural language from the Firmngin dashboard or connected messaging channels.

## Get started

In the Firmngin dashboard, open **AI Agents**, then configure the agent for your workspace:

- Choose a model
- Set instructions
- Grant tool permissions
- Connect [messaging channels](/channels) such as Telegram or Discord

<Note>
  Your plan must include AI credits, and your workspace must have enough remaining credit balance to run agent requests.
</Note>

## How credit billing works

Firmngin charges **flat AI credits per successful agent request**, not per token.

| What happens | Credit charge |
| --- | --- |
| First successful model turn for a user message | Flat credits for the selected model |
| Tool-loop / continuation turns for the same request | `0` additional credits |
| Failed request before a successful charge | No credit deducted for that charge attempt |

Token counts may still be recorded for analytics and usage history. Customer billing for AI Agents uses the flat credit amount only.

## Insufficient credits

If the workspace does not have enough AI credits for the selected model, Firmngin rejects the request and does not complete the agent turn.

To continue:

1. Check your remaining AI credit balance in the dashboard.
2. Buy more AI credits, or wait for included plan credits to renew.
3. Or switch to a model with a lower credit cost.
