Plans define how much of the firmngin platform your workspace can use: connected devices, monthly device messages, merchant profiles, data history, AI credits, concurrent AI tasks, and support level.

The exact price and quota for your workspace are shown on the Plans page in the dashboard. This page explains the plans in simple terms.

## Plan overview

| Plan | Short description |
| --- | --- |
| Hobby | For testing, prototypes, and small personal projects. |
| Maker | For small businesses or early production devices. |
| Pro | For growing teams that need more devices, longer data history, more merchants, and priority support. |
| Grow Up | For scaling businesses with larger fleets, more merchant capacity, and direct support. |
| Custom | For teams that need a tailored quota, custom pricing, or commercial terms that do not fit the standard plans. |

## What each plan controls

| Area | What it means for users |
| --- | --- |
| Devices | How many devices you can register and operate in the workspace. |
| Device messages | How much device activity your workspace can process each billing period. |
| Data history | How long recent device data is kept available in the platform. |
| Merchants | How many monetization merchant profiles your workspace can manage. |
| AI credits | Included AI usage credits, when AI features are enabled for the plan. |
| Concurrent AI tasks | How many AI tasks can run at the same time. |
| Support | The support channel or priority level included with the plan. |

## What is charged

- The selected paid plan is charged based on the billing cycle shown at checkout, such as monthly or yearly.
- Custom plans may include custom pricing for extra devices, extra device messages, or special commercial limits.
- Extra AI usage may require credits or paid terms when AI credit billing is enabled for your workspace.
- Payment provider or transaction fees for monetization flows are separate from the workspace subscription.

## What is not charged separately

- Creating devices, merchants, dashboards, or automations within your included plan limits.
- Device messages while your workspace stays within the included message quota.
- Data history while it stays within the retention included in your plan.
- Support that is already included in your selected plan.
- Viewing dashboards, device pages, logs, and plan usage inside the dashboard.

## How limits work

When a workspace reaches a plan limit, firmngin may stop accepting more of that resource until the quota resets, the usage is reduced, or the workspace upgrades to a larger plan.

Examples:

- If the device limit is reached, add a larger plan before registering more devices.
- If the monthly message quota is reached, device activity may be limited until the next billing period or an upgrade.
- If merchant capacity is reached, upgrade before creating more merchant profiles.

<Note>
  The dashboard is the source of truth for your active plan, current billing cycle, and available quota.
</Note>

## Choosing a plan

- Choose Hobby for experiments and early tests.
- Choose Maker for a small production setup.
- Choose Pro when you need more operational room and stronger support.
- Choose Grow Up when the workspace is scaling across more devices or business units.
- Choose Custom when your fleet, usage, or billing terms need a tailored agreement.
